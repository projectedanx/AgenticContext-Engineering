# Emergence Inversion Protocol: Plan

## 1. Thesis
The intersection of Human and AI capabilities yields value neither can achieve alone.
- **Human Value:** High-entropy input, epistemic dissonance, definition of contradictory Non-Functional Requirements (NFRs), and historical 'Scars' (lived failure modes).
- **AI Value:** Low-entropy structural enforcement, high-dimensional topological mapping (DAGs), constraint satisfaction, and Draft-Conditioned Constrained Decoding (DCCD).

## 2. Inversion Strategy
The standard paradigm (Human specifies solution -> AI builds solution) is structurally brittle. We invert this:
1. **Friction Capture:** Human specifies the *contradictions* and *scars* (e.g., "Must be completely decoupled but share a single transaction context").
2. **Paraconsistent Mapping:** AI accepts the contradiction without premature resolution, mapping the problem using a `FuzzyRCC-8` spatial calculus.
3. **Latent Emergence:** AI generates the architectural latent space where both conditions are structurally true, producing a `Pluriversal_Knowledge_Capsule`.

## 3. Agentic Features
1. **Dissonance Input Vectors:** Specialized UI modules for capturing contradictory goals (Vector A vs. Vector B).
2. **Paraconsistent Resolver Protocol:** AI routines that map the intersection of contradictions rather than averaging them out.
3. **Emergent Feature Extrusion:** Translation of the resolved latent space into actionable DCCD schemas.
