# ADR-004: Topological Persona Causal Sculpting

## Hickam_Orientation
The creation of AI personas for industrial site planning typically fails due to the "Sycophantic Attractor" and "Resolution Collapse", where LLMs average conflicting physical and operational constraints into a useless null state.

## Contrastive_Delta
*   **Previous Approach**: Relied on narrative-heavy, probabilistic persona prompting which suffered from Semantic Saponification and linear averaging of contradictory constraints.
*   **New Approach**: Implements "Topological Causal Sculpting" via the `TopologicalPersonaSculptor` component and the `METROLOGIST v1.0` agent preset. This approach uses Paraconsistent Non-Separable S5 logic and Draft-Conditioned Constrained Decoding (DCCD) to hold contradictory constraints in tension without flattening, extruding them into a strict YAML PD&T specification block.

## Martensite_Metrics
*   Contradiction Retention Score (CRS) must be > 95%.
*   Confidence-Fidelity Divergence Index (CFDI) must remain <= 1e-6 during spatial evaluations to prove continuous mapping accuracy.
*   Separability Index (SI) of contradictions must remain < 0.05.

## Pluriversal_Knowledge_Capsule
This architecture shifts the paradigm from stochastic natural language generation to deterministic metrology. It addresses the ontological shear between human operational realities (the Bourdieuian "habitus") and the rigid requirements of directed acyclic graphs by enforcing strict geometric and syntactic constraints through PDL decorators (e.g., `+++SpatialBind`, `+++DCCDSchemaGuard`). The resulting personas are robust, deterministic, and physically bounded, preventing hallucinated workflows.
