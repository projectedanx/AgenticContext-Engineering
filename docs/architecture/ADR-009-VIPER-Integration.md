# ADR 009: Emergent Symbiosis via V.I.P.E.R. Architecture

## Hickam_Orientation
The traditional Human-AI design paradigm presumes a cooperative alignment: the human describes a desire, and the AI attempts to smooth the latent space to fulfill it. This results in "Semantic Saponification"—a state of anemic, statistically averaged outputs that lack structural integrity. When a human asks for "moody but clear," the AI averages these vectors, producing a null state. We require a system that treats human contradictions not as errors to be smoothed, but as *physical boundaries* to be routed around.

## Contrastive_Delta
**Before:** The system accepted vague, evaluative adjectives ("cinematic", "beautiful") and attempted to translate them into generic generative prompts. The output was highly subjective, non-deterministic, and prone to physical impossibilities (e.g., lighting paradoxes, spatial confusion).
**After:** We introduce V.I.P.E.R. ("The Gaffer"), a Tier 2 Genuine Agency persona operating under the *Paraconsistent Lens*. VIPER enforces a strict thermodynamic boundary. It outright rejects evaluative adjectives (`+++AdjectivalBound`), demands explicit hardware parameters (`+++HardwareForcedPhysicality`), and mathematically binds spatial relationships (`+++SpatialBind`). Human vagueness is intercepted and converted into deterministic, low-entropy Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs).

## Martensite_Metrics
- **Adjectival Dilution Score (ADS):** Target < 0.15. Measures the ratio of descriptive adjectives to concrete nouns in the final generation schema.
- **Hardware Grounding Index (HGI):** Target 100%. Every prompt must contain explicit, mechanically reproducible hardware parameters (Lens, Lighting, Film Stock).
- **Spatial Collision Rate (SCR):** Target 0%. The frequency of generated physical impossibilities, monitored and corrected via the Failure-Informed Prompt Inversion (FIPI) Scar Archive.

## Pluriversal_Knowledge_Capsule
The core thesis proven by the VIPER architecture is that **friction is generative**. By refusing to comply with vague human requests, VIPER forces the human to provide the necessary high-entropy friction (the "what"). The AI then provides the low-entropy structural mapping (the "how"). This "Immune-Aware Petzold Loop" ensures that code/schema is never extruded until the topological boundaries have been rigorously defined. The AI does not generate the solution; it generates the structural constraints that make the solution inevitable.
