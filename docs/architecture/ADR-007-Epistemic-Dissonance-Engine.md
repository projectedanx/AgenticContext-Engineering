# ADR 007: Epistemic Dissonance Engine & Paraconsistent Routing

## Hickam_Orientation
The Agentic Context Engineering Workbench requires a deterministic protocol to harness the dialectical tension between high-entropy human intent (Scars, contradictions) and low-entropy AI structural enforcement (DAG routing, constraint satisfaction). The existing paradigm of direct declarative prompting suffers from Semantic Saponification and premature conflict resolution, yielding suboptimal, brittle architectures. We propose the **Epistemic Dissonance Engine**, an inversion strategy that weaponizes human-defined contradictory Non-Functional Requirements (NFRs) as the literal geometric boundary conditions for the AI's generation manifold.

## Contrastive_Delta
*   **Previous State:** Human provides declarative instructions; AI attempts to directly satisfy them, often averaging out contradictions into mediocrity (Semantic Saponification). Conflicts are resolved via manual human intervention prior to prompt execution.
*   **Proposed State:** Human provides explicit contradictions and historical "Scars" via the **Friction Ingestion Schema**. The AI utilizes a **Paraconsistent Lens** (operating within a `FuzzyRCC-8` spatial calculus framework) to hold the contradiction simultaneously. The AI generates the structural architecture (DAG) strictly within the negative space of the failure constraints.
*   **Differential Impact:** Yields "Emergent Resolution"—the discovery of the latent architectural space where both contradictory constraints are structurally valid, completely bypassing premature compromise.

## Martensite_Metrics
*   **Epistemic Escrow:** > 0.85 (Measure of retained contradiction prior to structural resolution).
