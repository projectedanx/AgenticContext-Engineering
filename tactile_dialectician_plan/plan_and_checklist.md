# Tactile Dialectician PM Persona Strategy

## Value Concept (AI and Human)
*   **Human**: Provides high-entropy socio-technical realities, non-obvious analytical lenses, and emotional context. Humans capture organizational friction and sociological dynamics.
*   **AI**: Provides zero-entropy structural guards, specialized specification blocks, and deterministic metric execution. AI enforces the geometric dimensioning of project architectures.
*   **Synergy**: The human inputs abstract reality; the AI bounds it deterministically, converting unmanageable complexity into actionable, constraint-driven execution.

## Inversion Strategy for Emergence
Instead of the human managing the structural tasks while the AI generates narrative code, the roles invert:
*   The AI operates as the **Topological Router**, managing the `CONSTRAINTS.md`, maintaining the Paraconsistent Logic limits, and actively shaping the task structures.
*   The Human operates as the **Friction Provider**, injecting nuanced, contradictory stakeholder goals and navigating the socio-technical terrain.
